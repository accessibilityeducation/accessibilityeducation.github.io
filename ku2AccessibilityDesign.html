Methods and approaches



Persona Co-Design for Improving Digital Accessibility

Research paper. "This paper examines approaches to developing authentic accessibility personas that can help improve the design of products for persons with disabilities..."

Co-creating Persona Scenarios with Diverse Users Enriching Inclusive Design

"In this article, we will examine personas as methodological approach and review some critiques about how its use may omit or stereotype users with disabilities or even restrict user involvement. We review previous persona creation methods and compare it to our approach where we involve diverse users directly in the personas creation process, to ensure more grounded personas."

Creating Personas with Disabilities

"We review the current state of the art of the personas and review some research and industry projects that use them. We outline techniques that can be used to create personas with disabilities. This includes advice on how to get more information about assistive technology and how to better include people with disabilities in the persona creation process"

Using Personas as an Inclusive Design Tool for Accessibility

53 minutes webinar on YouTube. "Denis Boudreau and Caitlin Geier, two of our UX accessibility experts, will talk about creating personas that you can use to promote inclusive design and accessibility for websites and applications."

Assistive Technology Users: Understanding the Representative Range
